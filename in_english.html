What is Laama Tech?

Laama Tech is a growing game development company, which specializes in 2D-platformers. Our company was established in October 2017, and we employ nearly 11 finnish IT-experts. Our estimated revenue for 2017 is 15 000 euros (student allowance paid by Kela [until Kela sees this and takes away our allowance]).

We want to offer memorable gaming experiences, superior to those by any other game developer. To accomplish this we have gathered together a team of the most talented game developers in Finland.
