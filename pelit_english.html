Über Dario Bros.

Our company's first game.
Deranged Browser has abducted the niece of our hero Dario. Dario and his brother, Un-Luigi go on a nutty adventure around the people's republic of Jäkälä to save his niece.

Über Dario Bros. achieved great success in Savo and Pori, winning the prestigious "Southwest Finlands game of the year" award.

Sanic: S I I L I

Tax haven Mokano has gotten into a bad debt spiral. Luckily for them, the end of the rainbow has been found, and people are gossiping about its fortunes constantly.

The leaders of Mokano decide to send their best debt collector Sanic to collect as much fortune as possible from the newly found cornucopia.

Hyper Miäs

The year is 7325. The elective oligarchic technocracy of Southwest Finland is in war with feodalic kleptomaniac theocracy of East Finland. One day, robots made for military use turn against their programming and start to clean up on both frontlines instead of fighting. War seems to be over, or at least a truce appears to be in place.

Hyper Miäs, the most elite fighter of the elective oligarchic technocracy of Southwest Finland, is ordered to infiltrate the feodalic kleptomaniac theocracy of East Finland to destroy the enemys robot control systems. But unsuspectingly, the feodalic kleptomaniac theocracy of East finland is planning to do the same with their master infiltrator, Ryä-äijä.

Hyper Miäs was nominated for the best story in Turun Sanomat newspaper's monthly attachment.
